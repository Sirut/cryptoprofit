How it works Crypto Profit?

This page is useful for speeding up the profit/loss calculation of your trade orders.

Input Parameters

  • Invested Capital: It refers to the total capital invested in the currency, in this field you can enter any FIAT currency (ex: dollars, euros, yen etc..)

  • Purchase Price: The purchase price is the value of the coin at the time of purchase. You can enter any price of each cryptocurrency.

  • Selling Price: This value refers to the price you have sold, or would like to sell your coin. Useful for calculating subsequent values.

Output Parameters

  • Quantity: The amount of coins purchased.

  • Difference: The difference between the purchase price and the sale price.

  • Percentage: The percentage of gain/loss.

  • Profit: Total value of net income (invested capital is subtracted).

  • Total: Total value of gross income (invested capital is not subtracted).
